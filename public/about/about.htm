Who We Are

We are a group of college students reaching out to individuals who are seeking to improve their health lifestyles by providing a tool to keep track of their progress.

What We Do

Profile

We've given the user a profile in which they can review their information and calculate their daily BMR.

Public Chat

A public chat so users can exchange ideas about exercise and diet plans.

Achievements

A set of minor goals the user can strive for while maintaining their exercise and diet plans.
